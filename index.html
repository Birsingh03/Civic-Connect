Building Community, Fostering Responsibility

Civic Connect is a web-based platform that allows citizens to report civic issues
such as garbage dumps, potholes, broken street lights, and water leaks in their locality. Users can upload a photo of the issue along with its location and description. The complaint is then forwarded to the concerned authorities for action.

About Civic Connect


  • 🌟 Why Civic Connect exists



    Civic Connect was created to provide a simple, transparent, and efficient way for citizens to report civic issues and ensure they are addressed by the concerned authorities. It aims to strengthen communication, accountability, and community involvement in maintaining public spaces.

  • 🚀 Our Mission



    Our mission is to empower citizens to take an active role in improving their communities by providing a user-friendly platform for reporting and tracking civic issues. We strive to enhance transparency, accountability, and responsiveness in local governance.

  • 🌐 Our Vision



    Our vision is to build smarter and cleaner cities by strengthening collaboration between citizens and authorities through technology, transparency, and accountability.

Features


  • 📍 Report Issues in Seconds



    Easily report civic problems like garbage dumps, potholes, broken street lights and water leakage by
    uploading a photo and short description.

  • 🗺️ Automatic Location Detection



    The app automatically captures your location so authorities know exactly where the issue exists — no need to explain directions.

  • 📊 Real-Time Complaint Tracking



    Track the progress of your complaint from Pending → In Progress → Resolved directly inside the app.

  • 👥 Community Participation



    Citizens can view, support and comment on issues reported by others, helping highlight important problems faster.

  • 🚨 Priority Based Resolution



    Problems reported by multiple people automatically gain higher priority so serious issues are solved first.

  • 🌍 Transparent Governance



    Everyone can see reported issues in their area, increasing accountability and encouraging faster action.
